Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingredients:
Bread (2 slices)
Peanut Butter
Jelly

Directions:
1. Spread 2 tbsp peanut butter on the first slice of bread using a knife.
2. Spread 1 tbsp jelly on the second slice of bread using a knife.
3. Close the two slices of bread into a sandwich (peanut butter and jelly sides on the inside)
4. Enjoy!
